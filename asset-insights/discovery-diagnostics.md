    # Discovery diagnostics

    Diagnostics and guardrail content includes troubleshooting IP Address with Discovery Diagnostics, prerequisites for provider discovery, provider-specific limitations, required permissions, service ports, and edit/delete configuration workflows.

## Triage checklist

* Confirm provider credentials and permissions.
* Review source-specific limitations.
* Check service ports and polling configuration.
* Validate destination, exclusions, and scope.
* Use discovery diagnostics to inspect specific IP addresses or missing assets.
